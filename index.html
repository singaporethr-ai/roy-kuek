Meet the Founder

Roy Kuek

Founder & Business Leader

With decades of experience across multiple industries, Roy Kuek has built a portfolio of companies grounded in one enduring principle: do things right, and do them with integrity.

A calm and deliberate strategist, Roy approaches business the way a craftsman approaches his work — with deep knowledge, steady hands, and a long view. His ventures span petrochemicals, sustainable packaging, and strategic trading across the Asia-Pacific region.

Beyond business, Roy is a passionate collector of fine Chinese antiques — an interest that reflects his appreciation for craftsmanship, history, and the value of things built to last.

"True success is not measured in speed, but in the depth of what you build and the trust you earn along the way."

The Collector

A Passion for the Timeless

Beyond the boardroom, Roy is a devoted collector of fine Chinese antique porcelain — a pursuit that began decades ago and has grown into a carefully curated personal collection of rare and museum-quality pieces.

Each piece in his collection carries a story — of dynasties, of craftsmen who devoted a lifetime to perfecting a single glaze, a single form. To Roy, collecting antiques is not merely about ownership. It is about preserving cultural memory and learning from the patience of those who came before us.

His collection spans blue and white dragon vases, famille rose reticulated porcelain, and celadon pieces from the Qing era — each chosen with the same quiet discernment he brings to every business decision.
